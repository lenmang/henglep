ABOUT HENGLEP

        
  • Henglep Village


    • Block / Subdivision → Churachandpur North

    District → Churachandpur

    State → Manipur

    According to Census 2011 information the location code or village code of Henglep village is 269426.
    Henglep village is located in Churachandpur North subdivision of Churachandpur district in Manipur, India.
    It is situated 80km away from district headquarter Churachandpur North.
    Churachandpur North is the sub-district headquarter of Henglep village.

    Henglep has a total population of 992 peoples, out of which male population is 504 while female population is 488.
    Literacy rate of henglep village is 82.66% out of which 85.12% males and 80.12% females are literate.
    There are about 173 houses in henglep village. Pincode of henglep village locality is 795128.

    Churachandpur is nearest town to henglep for all major economic activities, which is approximately 80km away.
